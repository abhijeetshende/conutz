GHUGNI | CHAAT | CHAKHNA

Conutz Recipes Variety

Conutz Bhel Chakna

Soaked and boiled conutz (proprietary indian corn , black and green chana, peanuts) with salt and severed with diced onion , tomato, green chilli and sprinkled spices..

Classic Boiled Conutz Chakhna

Soaked and boiled conutz (proprietary indian corn , black and green chana, peanuts) with salt and served.

Garlic Conutz Ghugni

Soaked and boiled conutz (proprietary indian corn , black and green chana, peanuts) sauteed in little oil along with onion, tomato ,green chilli, spices and topped with fried garlic cloves.

Kolhapuri Conutz Ghugni

Soaked and boiled conutz (proprietary indian corn , black and green chana, peanuts) sauteed in little oil along with onion, tomato ,green chilli and kolhapuri spices.

Mustard Green Chilli Conutz Ghugni

Soaked and boiled conutz (proprietary indian corn , black and green chana, peanuts) sauteed in little oil along with onion, tomato ,green chilli, spices and added mustard green chilli pickle to give flavour.

Shallow Fried Conutz Chakna

Soaked and boiled conutz (proprietary indian corn , black and green chana, peanuts) shallow fried in little oil and sprinkle salt ,spices and peppers.

Sweet And Sour Conutz Ghugni

Soaked and boiled conutz (proprietary indian corn , black and green chana, peanuts) sauteed in little oil along with onion, tomato ,green chilli and added tamarind chutney and spices to give sweet and sour flavour.

Traditional Conutz Ghugni

Soaked and boiled conutz (proprietary indian corn , black and green chana, peanuts) sauteed in little oil along with onion, tomato ,green chilli and spices.

Cheeze Conutz

Popcorn made fresh in kettle with cold press coconut oil to add reach flavour and sprinkle with himalayan pink salt and black peppers and served crunchy. The popcorn are made tiny but crunchy.
